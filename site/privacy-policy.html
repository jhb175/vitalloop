VitalLoop

Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: May 10, 2026

VitalLoop is a body status and daily coaching app for iPhone and Apple Watch. This policy explains what data the app uses, why it uses it, and how you can control it.

VitalLoop is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional health advice.

Data VitalLoop May Use

With your permission, VitalLoop may read summary data from Apple Health / HealthKit, including:

  • Activity energy, step count, and workout duration
  • Sleep duration
  • Resting heart rate and heart rate variability
  • Body weight

VitalLoop may also store information that you enter or record in the app, including:

  • Weight-loss or fitness goals
  • Apple Watch quick logs for stress, fatigue, and hunger
  • Daily summaries generated by the app
  • Manual weight entries and notes
  • Meal quick logs, meal categories, and meal notes

How Data Is Used

VitalLoop uses this data to generate local body status summaries, explainable scores, trends, and daily suggestions.

Health and body data is not used for advertising, marketing, or data mining.

Local-First Processing

The current version processes HealthKit data locally on your device. Raw HealthKit data is not uploaded to a cloud service by default.

HealthKit Permissions

VitalLoop only reads Apple Health data after you choose to connect Apple Health. You can change or revoke Health permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app under Health permissions for VitalLoop.

Data Deletion

You can delete VitalLoop's local records from the app's Settings and Privacy screen. This deletes local goals, daily summaries, subjective check-ins, weight records, and meal quick logs stored by VitalLoop. It does not delete source data from Apple Health.

Data Sharing

VitalLoop does not sell your personal data. The current version does not share raw HealthKit data with third parties.

Children

VitalLoop is not designed for children and should not be used as a medical monitoring tool.

Changes

This policy may be updated as VitalLoop adds features. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
