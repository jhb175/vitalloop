Support

Help for VitalLoop on iPhone and Apple Watch.

For beta feedback or support requests, use the GitHub issue tracker: github.com/jhb175/vitalloop/issues

For TestFlight feedback, use the structured beta form: submit beta feedback.

Before Contacting Support

  • Confirm the iPhone and Apple Watch apps are from the same TestFlight build.
  • Open both apps once if Apple Watch sync has not started.
  • Check iPhone Settings in VitalLoop for HealthKit and Watch sync diagnostics.
  • Do not attach raw HealthKit exports or sensitive health records.

Health And Safety

VitalLoop is not a medical device. Scores and suggestions are for lifestyle reference only and do not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional health advice.
