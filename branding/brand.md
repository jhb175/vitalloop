# VitalLoop Brand Draft

## Name

VitalLoop

## Positioning

VitalLoop is a body-signal coach that turns Apple Watch and iPhone data into daily decisions.

中文定位：

VitalLoop 是一个身体信号教练，把 Apple Watch 和 iPhone 的身体数据转成每天能执行的建议。

## Tagline

Body signals. Daily decisions.

中文辅助表达：

读懂身体信号，安排今天的行动。

## Logo Concept

- Outer ring: body data, goals and daily plans form a continuous feedback loop.
- Middle ring: activity, recovery and sleep are separate but connected signals.
- Inner ring: today's status score, kept secondary to the larger loop.
- Center loop: a compact inner feedback loop, avoiding the task-completion meaning of a checkmark.
- Rounded dark icon: matches the current deep Liquid Glass interface direction.

Revision note:

The first draft was too busy and left too much unclaimed space. The current version removes the heart and pulse line, enlarges the circular mark, and uses three clean rings as the primary visual language.

Second revision note:

The decorative dot near the V made the center mark feel like a checkmark with an unrelated accent. It has been removed. The three arc openings are now staggered more deliberately so the symbol feels like one arranged system rather than separate circular charts.

Third revision note:

The center V still read too much like a checkmark, which can suggest task completion instead of body monitoring. It has been replaced with a small continuous loop, reinforcing the VitalLoop concept without adding extra icon language.

Fourth revision note:

The white center loop had too much contrast and pulled attention away from the circular system. It has been replaced with a muted mint-blue core ring that stays within the same visual language as the outer rings.

Fifth revision note:

Two empty zones were visible around the center and lower-right interior. The mark now adds two secondary signal arcs: a compact core-status arc near the center and a short warm-violet auxiliary arc on the right side. Both stay in the circular system and avoid becoming standalone decoration.

Sixth revision note:

The lower-right auxiliary arc felt like a patch rather than part of the system. It has been removed. The mark now keeps only the compact core-status arc as a secondary detail.
